SCIENTIFIC CAUTION

What the platform does not assume

  • FIRMS detections are satellite thermal anomalies, not local air-temperature sensors.
  • Ambient weather is state-centre context unless a local station is explicitly shown.
  • Cause and smoke exposure require local confirmation.
The ambient apparent-temperature screen combines current temperature, humidity and wind. It excludes direct solar radiation and is not an official warning. The combined attention score is a transparent visualisation index, not an emergency classification.
